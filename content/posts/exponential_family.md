\begin{equation}
q(\theta) \propto \mathrm{exp}\left[ \lambda^T T(\theta) \right]
\end{equation}

where \\(\lambda\\) is the natural parameters, and \\(\theta\\) is the
sufficient statistics.

Expectation parameters:

\begin{equation}
  \mu := E\_q\left[T(\theta)\right]
\end{equation}
